RPS combines a **durability-first planning system** with an **AI coach** that has access to both your plan and your training data. That means guidance isn’t generic: the coach can interpret your recent workload, trends, and execution context to help you make smarter decisions—especially around building performance **under fatigue**, not just when you’re fresh.

The differentiator is the **closed-loop coaching experience**: planning, load control, and adaptation are connected in one workflow. The AI coach translates durability principles into actionable adjustments—when to push, when to consolidate, how to place intensity, and how to keep long-ride performance progressing without accumulating hidden fatigue. In short: RPS doesn’t just schedule workouts; it **coaches the outcome**—durable fitness that holds up late in the ride and across demanding weeks.
