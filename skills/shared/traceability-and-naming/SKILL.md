Apply these conventions:
- Every persisted artefact must remain traceable to upstream inputs.
- Use canonical naming and type labels expected by the workspace schemas.
- Keep data-confidence claims proportional to the evidence available.
- Preserve explicit references to selected week/range and upstream artefacts.
