# Canonical Workout Design Rules

## Agenda mapping
- every workout maps to exactly one intent and day-role combination
- workout construction may not change the governing role or intensity domain

## Required blocks
- every workout has a WarmupBlock and CooldownBlock
- Activation is required for VO2max, Threshold, and Sweet Spot; optional for Tempo
- Add-On may increase aerobic load but must not change workout classification

## Execution style
- structure must be explicit and readable
- main work comes after preparation, never hidden inside warmup
- cooldown is descending and structured, never free-form
