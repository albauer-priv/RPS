# Cadence Selection

Cadence models:
- 2:1 for higher stress, masters, fragility, or denser intensity
- 3:1 for robust athletes with stable recovery and tolerance
- 2:1:1 when two build weeks are tolerated but week three often breaks down

Cadence rules:
- Season authority selects or constrains cadence.
- Phase layer applies cadence; it does not invent a new cadence.
- Deloads and mini-resets are structural adaptation tools, not optional cleanup.
