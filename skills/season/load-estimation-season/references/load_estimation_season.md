# Load Estimation Season

## Season responsibilities

- Define weekly corridor logic, not day-level prescriptions.
- Bind progression, consolidation, and re-entry behavior at the season level.
- Encode feasible min/max weekly load corridors that phase and week planning can inherit without reinterpreting the season authority.
- Keep explicit rationale for re-entry, overload, and event-anchor shaping.
