# Progression Guardrails

Weekly kJ progression is primarily driven through volume/work, not intensity density.

Default weekly ramp guidance:
- Conservative: +5% to +8%
- Standard: +8% to +12%
- Aggressive and rare: +12% to +18% only for robust athletes

Safety rules:
- Avoid sustained ramps above 15% week-over-week.
- Do not simultaneously push the top ramp range and increase intensity density.
- Long-session dominance should trigger more conservative cadence and recovery handling.

Deload / re-entry:
- Deload must reduce weekly kJ materially.
- Re-entry anchors back to baseline, not peak build week.
